=== Z80 flags notation

[cols="1,4,1",id="_stacknotationforz80flags"]
.Z80 flags notation
|===
| Symbol | Description          | bit

| Fc     | Carry flag           | 0
| Fh     | Half Carry flag      | 4
| Fn     | Add/Subtract flag    | 1
| Fp     | Parity/Overflow flag | 2
| Fs     | Sign flag            | 7
| Fz     | Zero flag            | 6
|===
